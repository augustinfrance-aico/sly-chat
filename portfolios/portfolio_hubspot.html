One Specific Thing I Built in HubSpot
"Tell me one specific thing you've built in HubSpot and what problem it solved."
I built a B2B lead qualification and automated follow-up system for a prospecting pipeline targeting 100+ businesses per week. The problem: leads were being scraped and enriched but nobody was following up consistently — some got contacted 3 times, others got forgotten entirely.

What I built: A pipeline with lifecycle stage automation — every new contact got auto-scored (0-100) based on data completeness, industry match, and company size. High-scoring leads triggered an immediate task for the sales rep + an automated intro email sequence. Low scorers went into a nurture workflow. Duplicate contacts were merged automatically using email + company domain matching.

Result: Zero leads falling through cracks, 3x faster first-contact time, and the team stopped arguing about "whose lead is this."
