### The Numbers

- Book priced at $12.99: $9.10 per sale
- 100 sales/month: $910/month per book
- 5 books in related niches: $4,550/month
- Time to maintain: 0 hours after launch

### How to Do It in 10 Days
**Days 1-2:** Research a niche with high demand and low competition
Use: Amazon search bar auto-complete, BSR numbers, review count analysis

**Days 3-7:** Generate the full manuscript
Use Claude or ChatGPT with a structured prompt for each chapter:

**Day 10:** Create cover and publish
Use: Canva (cover), KDP dashboard (publish)

### The Compound Effect

Book 2 launches in week 3. Royalties compound.
By month 6: 5 books generating income simultaneously.
